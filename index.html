"The goal of every module here is simple: transfer visual intuition. Before you can use a formula, you need to feel it. Before you can apply an algorithm, you need to see it breathe. These tools exist to close the gap between abstract theory and lived, embodied understanding."

// how these were built
AI Generation — 80%
Human — 20%

Every module was built through a human-AI collaboration. Roughly 80% of the code, animations, and content structure was generated by large language models acting as tireless co-authors. The remaining 20% represents human curation: the choice of what to teach, the pedagogical sequencing, the insight into what actually confuses learners, and the judgment calls that no model can make alone. This is a living experiment in what becomes possible when deep domain expertise is amplified by AI.
